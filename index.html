Protect yourself from Coronavirus

  • Wash Your Hands Often
  • Maintain Social Distancing
  • Wear Face Masks while going out
  • Stay home if you are sick
  • Clean and Disinfect

Wash Your Hand

  • Wash your hands often with soap and water for atleast 20 seconds.

  • Alternatively use a hand sanitizer that contains atleast 60% alcohol.

  • Avoid touching your eyes,nose and mouth with unwashed hands.

Practice Social Distancing

  • Avoid contact with people who are sick.

  • Whenever outdoors keep atleast six-feet distance from the next person.

Wear A Face Mask

  • Whenever outdoors wear a face mask.

  • Caregivers should compulsarily wear face masks and gloves when attending patients.

Stay Home

  • Stay home if you are sick except if want to visit a doctor.

  • If you must cough or sneeze,cover your mouth and nose with inside of elbow or use a tissue paper and dispose it properly.

  • Keep your pet animals clean and healthy.

Clean And Disinfect

  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces.This includes tables,doorknobs,light switches,handles,desks,phones,keyboards,toilets and sinks.

  • Clean the floors with disinfectant.Use disinfectant spray in rooms.
